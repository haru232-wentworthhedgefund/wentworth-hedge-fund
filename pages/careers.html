POSITIONS

Career opportunities across our business platform.

Below are core roles aligned with Wentworth Hedge Fund’s business verticals. Each role is designed to support institutional-quality research, disciplined execution, and long-duration value creation.

Asset Management

Asset Management Analyst

This role supports portfolio construction, investment monitoring, and risk-aware capital allocation across public market opportunities. The analyst works closely with senior investment professionals to evaluate company fundamentals, valuation signals, portfolio exposures, and cross-asset developments.

  • Assist with portfolio research, screening, and investment memos.
  • Monitor performance attribution and position-level risk factors.
  • Support long-term stewardship through disciplined reporting.
Apply for this role
Advisory

Financial Strategy Consulting Associate

This position supports strategic advisory work related to financing conditions, balance sheet priorities, and capital planning. Associates help translate macro and market developments into practical decision frameworks for institutional and corporate stakeholders.

  • Prepare strategy materials, briefing packs, and analytical frameworks.
  • Assess capital structure questions and market-sensitive planning needs.
  • Support client-ready presentations with clear, structured recommendations.
Apply for this role
Research

Market Research Associate

The Market Research Associate conducts macro, sector, and company-level analysis to support investment judgment and institutional planning. The role requires strong synthesis skills, careful data interpretation, and the ability to transform raw information into concise, decision-useful insight.

  • Track policy, industry, and company developments across markets.
  • Draft research summaries, thematic briefs, and structured market updates.
  • Support internal and external reporting with evidence-based analysis.
Apply for this role
Japan Focus

Japanese Equity Investment Analyst

This role focuses on listed Japanese companies and supports bottom-up equity research with a strong understanding of domestic policy, valuation asymmetries, governance developments, and sector-level opportunity sets. The analyst contributes directly to idea generation and investment surveillance.

  • Build company models, earnings views, and valuation frameworks.
  • Track management quality, governance trends, and industry catalysts.
  • Support Japanese market strategy with fundamental conviction.
Apply for this role
Cross-Border

Global Trade Intelligence Analyst

The Global Trade Intelligence Analyst tracks international logistics, commodity routes, policy shifts, and commercial linkages that shape the operating backdrop for cross-border investment and business activity. The role blends macro sensitivity with practical commercial awareness.

  • Monitor shipping trends, trade frictions, and supply-chain developments.
  • Analyze regional policy changes and cross-border economic implications.
  • Produce actionable intelligence on global commercial conditions.
Apply for this role
Operations

Institutional Travel Coordinator

This role supports executive and institutional travel planning linked to meetings, due diligence, conferences, and international business activity. The coordinator ensures that travel execution is efficient, professional, and aligned with the firm’s broader relationship management standards.

  • Coordinate business travel schedules, booking flows, and itinerary planning.
  • Support investor meetings and cross-border business logistics.
  • Maintain high-touch service standards and execution accuracy.
